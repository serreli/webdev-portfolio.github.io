The importance of running

Running is a fundamental cardiovascular exercise that promotes heart health by enhancing circulation, reducing the risk of cardiovascular diseases, and improving overall cardiovascular endurance. Beyond its physical benefits, running also plays a crucial role in mental well-being, as it releases endorphins, reduces stress, and fosters a sense of accomplishment, contributing to a balanced and healthier lifestyle.
